The Customer Behaviour and Sales Analysis System is a web-based application designed to simplify and automate the management of sales, inventory, and customer data while providing meaningful insights into customer purchasing behaviour. The system consists of two main roles: Admin and Salesman. The admin is responsible for managing products, updating stock, monitoring sales, and analysing customer behaviour. The salesman handles the billing process by recording customer details and selling products. Initially, the admin adds product information such as product ID, name, price, and quantity into the system. This data is stored in a centralized database, ensuring easy access and management. Whenever new stock arrives, the admin can update the existing product quantity or add new products. The salesman uses the billing module to generate bills by entering customer details like name and age, selecting products, and specifying the quantity. Once a bill is generated, the system automatically updates the inventory by reducing the product quantity and storing the transaction details in the database. If a product is out of stock or insufficient quantity is available, the system alerts the salesman, preventing incorrect billing.All sales transactions and customer data are recorded and displayed on the admin dashboard. The system performs analysis on this data to identify patterns such as top-selling products, total revenue, and customer distribution based on age groups. These insights help the admin understand customer preferences and make informed business decisions, such as offering discounts to specific age groups or promoting popular products.
